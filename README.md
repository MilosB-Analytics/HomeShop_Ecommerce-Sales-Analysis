# Global E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes global e-commerce sales data using **Power Bi** to evaluate sales performance, customer demographics, product category trends, and year-over-year growth.

The goal of the analysis is to identify **key revenue drivers, customer segments, and product categories** contributing to overall business performance.

---

## Executive Summary

The analysis highlights **strong year-over-year sales performance**, with revenue steadily increasing throughout the year and peaking in **December with over 530K in sales**. Sales trends indicate a seasonal pattern, with growth accelerating in **Q4**, suggesting strong holiday-driven demand.

Customer demographic analysis shows that **Adults generate the majority of sales (~2.18M)**, significantly outperforming other age groups. **Young Adults (~1.04M)** and **Middle Age customers (~0.91M)** represent the next largest segments, while **Teenagers and Senior Citizens contribute a smaller share of total revenue**.

Product category performance reveals that **Electronics is the dominant revenue driver (~1.25M)**, followed by **Jewelry (~631K)** and **Automotive (~368K)**. Several mid-tier categories such as **Home & Garden, Sports, and Clothing** contribute moderate sales volume, while categories like **Books and Food & Grocery generate comparatively lower revenue**.

Geographic analysis indicates that the **United States is the company’s largest market**, significantly outperforming other countries. Secondary markets include the **United Kingdom, Germany, Canada, and Australia**, which contribute steady but smaller sales volumes.

Overall, the analysis demonstrates that the company’s growth is driven primarily by **strong US market performance, high-value product categories such as Electronics, and purchasing activity from adult customer segments**.

## Key Insights

### Customer Demographics
- **Adults generate the largest share of revenue (~2.18M)**.
- **Young Adults (~1.04M)** represent the second strongest segment.
- **Middle Age customers (~0.91M)** provide stable purchasing activity.
- **Teenagers and Senior Citizens contribute the smallest sales share**.

### Sales Trends (Current vs Previous Year)
- Sales show **steady growth throughout the year**.
- **December recorded the highest monthly revenue (~532K)**.
- Q4 demonstrates the **strongest seasonal performance**, indicating holiday demand impact.
- The **current year consistently performs slightly above the previous year**, indicating positive growth.

### Product Category Performance
Top performing categories:

| Category | Sales |
|--------|--------|
| Electronics | ~1.25M |
| Jewelry | ~631K |
| Automotive | ~368K |

Moderate performance categories include:
- Home & Garden
- Sports
- Clothing
- Health
- Software

Lower revenue categories include:
- Books
- Food & Grocery
- Music

### Geographic Performance
- **United States is the largest revenue contributor**.
- Secondary markets include:
  - United Kingdom
  - Germany
  - Canada
  - Australia
- Smaller markets include **France, India, and Japan**.

## Business Takeaways
- The **US market drives the majority of revenue**.
- **Adult consumers represent the most valuable customer segment**.
- **Electronics and high-value categories generate the strongest revenue streams**.
- Seasonal demand peaks in **Q4**, suggesting opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns and inventory planning.
